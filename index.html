About Me

Hello! I am Aditya. I am an engineer, and I got my B.E. in Electronics and Instrumentation from Birla Institute Of Technology & Sciences, Pilani - BITS Pilani in 2019. I had a chance to work at the Integrated Systems Lab at CSIR - Central Electronics Engineering Institute, Pilani under the guidance of Mr. Sumeet Saurav during my final year of undergraduate studies.

My interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, control systems, and embedded systems. I am particularly interested in developing intelligent autonomous systems for environmental and social problems. To get a better insight on my life as an academic so far, you can have a look at my CV CV

When I am not working, I spend my time watching science fiction movies and TV shows, and often play multiplayer video games. I have a strong curiosity to know the reason behind things, about how they are done, and why they are done they way they are. It makes me restless when I don't get the answer to those questions!
