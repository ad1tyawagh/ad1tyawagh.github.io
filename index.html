Aditya Wagh

Hello there! Yes you! I am Aditya. I did my B.E. in Electronics and Instrumentation from Birla Institute Of Technology & Sciences, Pilani (BITS Pilani) from 2015-2019. I had a chance to work at the Integrated Systems Lab at CSIR - Central Electronics Engineering Institute, Pilani under the guidance of Mr. Sumeet Saurav during my final year of undergraduate studies.

My interests are at the intersection of computer vision, control systems, robotics and embedded systems. I am particularly interested in developing intelligent autonomous systems for environmental and social problems.

I maintain a list of my projects under the Projects tab. You may also check out my Github profile.To get a better insight on my life as an academic so far, you can have a look at my curriculum vitae.

When I am not doing any "supposedly academic" stuff, I spend my time watching science fiction movies and TV shows, and often play multiplayer video games. I have a strong curiosity to know the reason behind things, about how they are done, and why they are done they way they are. It makes me restless when I don't get the answer to those questions!

I blog about things that I come across and find fascinating, which is here.
